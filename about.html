Who We Are?

Transocean Marine Surveyors (E.A.) Ltd was established with core business in sales, service and installation of marine electronics, communication and navigation equipment to the shipping industry. Our company has vast experience in Marine Electronics in Kenya, Zanzibar, Tanzania and Somalia. We sell & support all major brands for navigation, radar, fish finders, GPS, VHF & SSB radios.

No matter what size your vessel is our experts can help you select the appropriate electronics at affordable prices.
